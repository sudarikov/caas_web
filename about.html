Who we are

We are young and ambitious company which believes that most of the organizations can achieve outstanding results investing more into their culture and evolving leaders.

Over last 10 years our team observed, analyzed and influenced company cultures around the world. Those were companies of different scale and industries, but all of them had one thing in common - culture which drives performance, innovation and creativity.

We are sure that companies with great culture might turn it into competitive advantage which is almost impossible to copy. And we are here to help you do so!

Our primary expertise is engineering culture for high-tech companies. But a lot of similar principles can be applied to different areas where you still need passionate, happy and successful teams.
