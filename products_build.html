Build the Culture™

This product is designed to grow great teams from scratch and build solid basement for the future success.

This includes:

  • Understanding your values and align them with business strategy
  • Employer value-added proposition and your brand as an employer
  • Job descriptions to attract the right people. Help with hiring a great team
  • Help with on-boarding and get the team up to speed
  • Leadership mentoring and coaching - to ensure day-to-day behaviours are based on values
  • Communication - make sure the team is aligned with defined values
  • Develop rewards program aligned with values
  • Define success metrics and make sure they are aligned with values and business strategy
  • Define performance review process. Promotions strategy
  • Make sure everyone has great personal and professional development plan
