Have you ever thought why similar companies with similar resources available sometimes achieve very different results?

While there is no clear answer to this question, more and more executives around the world believe that company culture might be one of the key elements to make a difference.

If you want to attract top talents, make them stay long in your company, enable them to do right decisions based on the context, keep them happy and productive and as a result be ahead of competition - company culture is very important for you.

“If you hire people just because they can do a job, they’ll work for your money. But if you hire people who believe what you believe, they’ll work for you with blood, sweat, and tears.”
