Culture as Usual™

This service will be the best for the companies which have remote teams and don't want to setup HR department in remote locations.

Our culture consultants will execute full-cycle HR activities in remote location under steering from your onshore HR team:

  • Coordinate hiring activities
  • Do on-boarding of new members
  • Produce personal development plans from every member of the team
  • Track progress of the team and check where everyone is against her own goals regularly
  • Get feedback from the team and share it with you
  • Do annual or semi-annual performance reviews and compensation reviews
  • Make sure every person stays happy, productive and motivated
  • Constant HR support for your offshore team leaders
